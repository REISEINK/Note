
### KawaiiPhysics

#### FKawaiiPhysicsModule



evaluate_anythread
Called to evaluate local-space bones transforms according to the weights set up in [Update()](https://docs.unrealengine.com/4.27/en-US/API/Runtime/Engine/Animation/FAnimNode_Base/Update/index.html). You should implement either Evaluate or EvaluateComponentSpace, but not both of these. This can be called on any thread.
